Pasta

  • Time: 1 h
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Chiken 1
Potatoes 5
Onion 1
Cheese 200 g

Step 1: Prepare the arrabiata sauce: heat the olive oil in a deep frying pan, fry the chopped onion and garlic.

Step 2:Cut the chili peppers into thin rings and the sweet peppers into strips and add to the pan.

Step 3:Cook for 3 minutes. Mash the tomatoes with a fork and add to the pan with the juice, salt.

Step 4:Simmer for 5 minutes.

Step 5:Meanwhile, boil the capellini in booling salted water until tender.

Step 6:Cut the beef sirloin into thin slices, fry in olive oil for 4 minutes, season with salt and pepper. Mix the pasta, sauce and meat. Garnish the dish with basil leaves.

Sushi

  • Time: 3 h
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Rice 340 g
Cucamber 1
Salmon 1
Cheese 200 g

Step 1: Place a strip of nori on your hand, place rice on it, place a couple of rice on one edge of the nori so that the edges stick together and wrap the ball of rice in the nori.

Step 2:From half of the nori sheet, roll up the rolls. The filling is the same as in the case of the ginkanami. The only difference - since we make rolls from half a sheet, the strips of filling should be thin and no more than 3 components (for example: cucumber, Almette, shrimp), and rice lay out a thin layer - 2-3 rice. You can make rolls and the whole length, but I like small, for one bite.

Step 3:Put them in boiling oil. The oil should cover the rolls more than halfway, then they will not absorb the excess.

Step 4:Fry for 1-2 minutes until golden color on each side.

Step 5:Take it out, cut it into 8 pieces. Let's eat. I recommend everyone who has ever made rolls to try it! Crispy hot rolls with soft cheese inside are delicious.

Source: Povarenok

Cake

  • Time: 2 h
  • Serves: 5
Ingredients Quantity
Cookies 400 g
Butters 150 g
Cream 120 g
Cheese 800 g

Step 1: Melt the butter in the microwave. This process took me 45 seconds (at 800 watts).

Step 2: Pour the melted butter into the container with the cookies and mix the ingredients thoroughly.

Step 3: Tamp the mixture into the mold. The thickness of the bottom of the cheesecake - a little more than 10 mm , the sides - about 5-7 mm.

Step 4: Add 3 eggs, 200 g of sugar and a package of vanilla sugar (I have 10 g in the package). Again "grind" all the ingredients with a blender, trying to avoid excessive saturation of the curd mass with air - move the blender inside the mass, bringing it to the surface as rarely as possible.

Step 5: We send the cheesecake to the oven preheated to 170 degrees and leave it alone for 50 minutes. This is your own personal time. Cheesecake behaves quite predictably during 50 minutes - it does not spread, does not rise, does not burn. I do not recommend opening the oven while it is baking.

Step 6: Increase the oven temperature to 200 degrees. Pour the sour cream mixture over the cottage cheese mixture, if necessary, "smooth" it slightly with a spoon. Send the cheesecake into the oven heated to 200 degrees for 7 minutes.
